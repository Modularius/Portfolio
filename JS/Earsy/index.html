Earsy is an mobile web-based app to boost your foreign language ability. Using Earsy you train your ears to understand the spoken words of another language.

Introduction

The idea of Earsy is to get your brain used to hearing the sounds of a foreign language, dispensing entirely with spelling and text-based learning. By eschewing reading and writing skills we force your brain to learn vocabulary and grammar through sound, which is more practical for day-to-day use. You can choose to learn either through a Quick Lesson, which presents randomly selected sentences or phrases and asks you to identify them, or through the more structured lesson modules.
